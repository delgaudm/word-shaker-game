Word Shaker

How to play

  1. Everyone gets paper and a pencil. One person taps Shake the board and sets the phone in the middle of the table.
  2. When the dice settle, a 3-minute round begins. Write down every word you can find.
  3. Words are built from adjacent letters — sideways, up-and-down, or diagonal — each die used at most once per word. Words must be at least 3 letters.
  4. When the board dims, pencils down. Read your lists aloud: any word found by two or more players is crossed out by everyone.
  5. Score what survives: 3–4 letters = 1  ·  5 = 2  ·  6 = 3  ·  7 = 5  ·  8+ = 11

The Qu die counts as two letters. Underlined letters (M, W, N, Z) show which way is up.
